About Us

Who We Are

We are a passionate team committed to reducing food waste, enhancing food security, and fostering a sustainable, community-driven meal-sharing network. Our platform, the Community Kitchen Network, connects food donors, community kitchens, and food seekers through a seamless and technology-driven system.

Our Mission

To build an inclusive and scalable food redistribution system that ensures surplus food reaches those who need it most, minimizing waste and hunger.

Our Vision

We envision a world where no meal goes to waste and everyone has access to nutritious food through real-time coordination, efficient logistics, and responsible donations.

How It Works

  • Food donors (restaurants, stores, households) list surplus food.
  • Community kitchens and volunteers coordinate pickups and deliveries.
  • Food seekers access meals through the platform.
  • Technology ensures inclusivity, supporting both online and SMS-based access.

Our Impact

  • Ensuring nutritious food access for those in need.
  • Minimizing food waste from businesses and households.
  • Optimizing logistics for fast, safe food distribution.
  • Engaging communities through volunteer incentives.
